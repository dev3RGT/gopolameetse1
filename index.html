What We Do

Complete Water
Drilling Solutions

From initial site assessment to final pump installation — we handle every step of your water project.

Borehole Drilling

High-precision percussion and rotary borehole drilling for residential, commercial, and agricultural applications.

Site Assessment

Thorough geological surveys and hydrogeological studies to identify the best drilling sites before we break ground.

Pump Installation

Supply and installation of submersible pumps, surface pumps, and solar-powered pumping systems tailored to your yield.

Water Quality Testing

Comprehensive water analysis and testing services to ensure your borehole water is safe for drinking and irrigation use.

Maintenance & Repairs

Scheduled maintenance, emergency repairs, and rehabilitation of existing boreholes and pump systems.

Borehole Casing & Lining

Professional casing, sealing, and lining services to protect your borehole and ensure long-term water safety.
